Cyber Crime Laws in India

Overview

Cyber crime refers to illegal activities conducted using computers or the internet. It includes hacking, identity theft, phishing, and more. Here are some key points:

  • Cyber crimes are governed by the Information Technology Act, 2000.
  • Offenses include hacking, data theft, cyberstalking, and online fraud.
  • Victims can file complaints with cyber crime cells or police stations.

Key Laws

  • Section 66: Punishment for hacking and data theft.
  • Section 67: Punishment for publishing obscene material online.
  • Section 69: Powers to intercept, monitor, or decrypt information.
  • Section 72: Breach of confidentiality and privacy.

Steps to Report Cyber Crime

  • Gather evidence such as screenshots, emails, or chat logs.
  • File a complaint with the nearest cyber crime cell or police station.
  • Provide all necessary details, including your identity and evidence.
