The Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam Act

Overview

The Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam, 2023 (BSA) is an Act of the Parliament of India to consolidate and amend the law of evidence.

Key Features

  • Digital Evidence: BSA recognizes electronic records, emails, server logs, and other digital communications as admissible evidence.
  • Admissibility of Confessions: BSA outlines the conditions under which confessions made to police officers can be admitted as evidence.
  • Expert Opinions: BSA allows for the admissibility of expert opinions on matters of science, art, or foreign law.

Key Sections

  • Section 3: Interpretation clause.
  • Section 6: Relevancy of facts forming part of same transaction.
  • Section 17: Admission defined.
  • Section 45: Opinions of experts.
  • Section 65B: Admissibility of electronic records.

Importance of the Act

The Act is crucial for providing a modern legal framework for evidence, ensuring fair trials, and promoting justice.
